Wash Care Instructions

Ensure the longevity and appearance of our products with these wash care guidance.

Washing

Machine wash cold, inside-out, gentle cycle with mild detergent & similar colors.

Bleaching

Employ non-chlorine bleach only when essential.

Drying

Tumble dry low, or hang-dry in shade for the longest life, don’t dry clean.

Ironing

Cool iron inside-out if necessary. Do not iron on printed area.
